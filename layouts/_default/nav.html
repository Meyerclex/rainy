tulip's secluded garden

through the thick of the misty woods, you find a mysterious stone path, lined with small wildflowers and lit lanterns. after following it for some time, the mist begins to part, and you arrive at a small garden that has been meticulously tended to, filled with blooming flowers under the moonlight.

there is a signpost located just outside the old iron gates of the garden. a magic barrier prevents you from venturing forward until you read the sign...

garden rules:

this is a brief overview of the rules within this realm. more detailed information can be found in the server rules.

  1. be mindful and kind. hateful behavior is not tolerated here - no bigotry, no slurs, and so forth. additionally, please try to keep playful rudeness/aggression to a minimum. not everyone can easily tell real insults from jest, so please be mindful of that. also, if you're talking about something potentially triggering or sensitive, i'd encourage the use of spoiler tags formatted something like this: (cw: subject) ||subject goes here||, just so people can opt out from seeing something upsetting.

  2. prohibited subjects: illegal content, violence/gore, suicide/self-harm, harassment, spamming. on a less strictly prohibited level, i'd like to avoid overly flashing gifs and discussion of recreational drug usage (ex: alcohol, weed, etc), just because that can be a sensitive area for folks.

  3. please avoid heavy self-deprecation and venting, or bringing outside discourse into here. life is cruel and challenging, as we all know. but i am not a therapist or an organizer for social change. it's difficult to moderate, and can be triggering for folks. there are many other communities better suited for these subjects - but i am just one artist on the world wide web. that being said, if you have an issue with someone or something in this server, please don't be afraid to DM a mod about it.

  4. NO UNDERAGE FOLKS. this is strictly an 18+ adult space. i will politely kick minors out in the interest of their own safety. i will also not tolerate sexual content featuring minors.

  5. there is a dedicated 18+ channel, but there are specific guidelines involved. namely: no explicit porn allowed, it's primarily a space to post nsfw-adjacent work (ex: artistic nudity/lingerie) without focusing on explicit sexual content.
